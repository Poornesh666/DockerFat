About Computer Science Department

The Department of Computer Science is at the forefront of technological advancement, offering cutting-edge programs in software engineering, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and data science. With a focus on practical skills and research, our graduates are leaders in the tech industry.

Courses Offered

  • B.Sc. Computer Science

    Fundamental concepts in programming, algorithms, data structures, and computer systems. Duration: 4 years.

  • M.Sc. Artificial Intelligence

    Advanced topics in machine learning, neural networks, natural language processing, and robotics. Duration: 2 years.

  • Ph.D. Cybersecurity

    Research-oriented program focusing on network security, cryptography, ethical hacking, and digital forensics.

  • M.Tech. Software Engineering

    Practical training in software development lifecycle, agile methodologies, DevOps, and cloud computing. Duration: 4 years.
